Visualization

Get thorough analysis
of German Credit Data

  • Age distribution of people has been looked into following which we can conclude that most people who apply for loans lie in the age category 20-40
  • Another interesting find is that we can see the young adult group and the senior group stand a more chance of a loan approval compared to students and elderly people.
  • Just like age, we also look into the sex distribution, that is the male:female ratio for applying loans and getting approved.
  • When we look into the application reasons of a loan, we observe that most people take loans for retraining, repairs and television/radio purposes.
  • By looking at the violin graph, we can notice the distribution of good and bad credit among different category of employees.
  • Last but not the least, we can assess the levels of risk by wealth. We can observe that people in the poor category have a very high risk of getting rejected while the risk drops down dramatically for rich people.

Few Insights.

Using KNN clustering to improve prediction accuracy of Loan Approval

Dataset divided into clusters followed by classification on each of the clusters.

  • Clustering helped in improving the model accuracy by 3-5%.
  • After clustering, XGBoost model showed the highest improvement, which has been used in this website for loan prediction.
